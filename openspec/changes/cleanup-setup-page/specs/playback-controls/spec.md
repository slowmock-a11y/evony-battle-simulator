## REMOVED Requirements

### Requirement: Three playback modes
**Reason**: Playback buttons (Step, Round, Full Battle, Reset) are being removed from the Setup page. They clutter the configuration view and belong in the Battle View.
**Migration**: Playback controls will be relocated to Battle View in a future change. The Battle View toggle button remains available on the Setup page.

### Requirement: Playback speed control
**Reason**: The speed slider is being removed from the Setup page alongside the playback buttons it controls.
**Migration**: Speed control will be relocated to Battle View alongside the playback buttons in a future change.
